Cancer facilities

We have developed a comprehensive portal to profile cancer facilities across Uganda, highlighting the range of services they offer, including chemotherapy, imaging, diagnosis, radiation therapy, surgery, palliative care, counselling, support groups, rehabilitation services, complementary and alternative medicine (CAM), and survivorship programs. This initiative aims to improve access to quality cancer care, empower patients and healthcare providers with information, facilitate referrals, and foster collaboration among stakeholders.
